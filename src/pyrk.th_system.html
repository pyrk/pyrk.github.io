pyrk.th_system module¶

class pyrk.th_system.THSystem(kappa, components)[source]¶

Bases: object

This class models: - lumped capacitance model for slab geometry - 1-D heat diffusion in spherical geometry, for heat generation at any radius in the sphere, - advective heat transfer by fluid - convective heat transfer at the solid surface to fluid.
